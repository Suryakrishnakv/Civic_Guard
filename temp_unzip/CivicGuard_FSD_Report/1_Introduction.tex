\chapter{INTRODUCTION}

\section{Project Overview}
\textbf{CivicGuard} is a web-based platform designed to empower citizens to participate in urban maintenance. It provides a streamlined way for residents to report damages to public assets (such as broken streetlights, road damages, or overflowing bins) directly to the relevant municipal authorities.

\section{Problem Statement}
Current methods of reporting public asset damage are often fragmented, involve significant red tape, and lack transparency. Citizens often don't know who to contact, and even after reporting, they have no visibility into the progress of their complaint.

\section{Objectives}
\begin{itemize}
    \item To provide a user-friendly interface for reporting asset damage.
    \item To automate the routing of reports to the correct municipal departments.
    \item To improve transparency by allowing citizens to track the status of their reports.
    \item To provide administrative tools for monitoring and managing urban maintenance tasks.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{CivicGuard} is a web-based platform designed to empower citizens to participate in urban maintenance. It provides a streamlined way for residents to report damages to public assets (such as broken streetlights, road damages, or overflowing bins) directly to the relevant municipal authorities.

The system is built on the Laravel framework, providing a secure and efficient environment for data management and user interactions. Citizens can register, login, and submit detailed reports including photographs and location data. Each report is automatically routed to the corresponding municipal department (e.g., Engineering, Health & Sanitation, Water Supply) based on predefined categories, ensuring that issues are addressed by the right personnel without manual intervention.

Key features include a responsive citizen dashboard for tracking report statuses, administrative tools for department officers to manage and update tasks, and an notification system to keep users informed of progress. By digitizing and transparently managing public asset complaints, CivicGuard aims to improve the responsiveness of urban administration and foster a sense of community responsibility.
